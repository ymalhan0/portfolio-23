My Contribution
Towards the end of my internship, I was assigned a new project where I updated the *Share feature on *Predict to help brand managers understand the relative performance of their brands and determine the right tactic to optimize revenue potential.
*Share provides the data on market share percentage in a selected category.
*Predict is the data-driven eCommerce solution that helps brands succeed
What’s the user’s goal?
“As a brand manager, I want to obtain the market share position of my brands within the categories we sell in. While the Share feature on Predict provides me data on my share percentage in a selected category, it doesn't answer the questions: how is my brand's share growing relative to the overall category growth? how are my brand's competitors performing?

Solution
My goal was to improve the current Share feature by adding more details and ways to view data, ultimately answering the questions users had as to how they were doing in the market by providing more insight.
